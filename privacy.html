Privacy Policy — Fulove Media

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Fulove Media ("the App") is a private, single-operator publishing tool. This policy describes how data is handled.

  1. Who uses the App. Only the operator (Curtis Fu) uses the App. There are no third-party users, no accounts, no sign-ups.
  2. Data collected. The App stores OAuth access and refresh tokens issued by TikTok and other platforms solely to authenticate posting requests on the operator's own accounts. It processes video files and captions supplied by the operator. No personal data from any third party is collected.
  3. Data storage. Tokens are stored locally on the operator's machine. Video files are stored on the operator's private object storage. No data is shared with third parties.
  4. Data sharing. No data is sold, rented, or disclosed to any third party. The App communicates only with official platform APIs.
  5. Retention. Tokens are retained until revoked. Video files are deleted from storage after publishing.
  6. Security. Tokens and credentials are kept in local environment files with restricted file permissions.
  7. Your rights. As the sole user is the operator, standard data-subject requests are not applicable. The operator may revoke tokens and delete all local data at any time.
  8. Changes. This policy may be updated at any time.
  9. Contact. hello@fulove.media
